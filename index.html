About Me

I am a Professor with the School of Artificial Intelligence and Automation at Huazhong University of Science and Technology，China.

  • Postdoctoral Fellow, (2021-2024), Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Lakehead University, Canada.

  • Postdoctoral Associate, (2020-2021), Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Western University, Canada.

  • Ph.D., (2016-2020), Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Western University, Canada. (Supervisor Prof. Abdelhamid Tayebi)

  • M.Sc., (2014-2015), Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Lakehead University, Canada. (Supervisor Prof. Abdelhamid Tayebi)

  • B.Eng., (2009-2013), School of Automation, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China.

Research Interests

My research interests mainly focus on nonlinear estimation and control with applications to autonomous robotic systems:

  • Nonlinear state estimation for inertial navigation systems (INS)

  • Geometric control of unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs)

  • Robot navigation and obstacle avoidance

  • Coordination of multi-robot systems
